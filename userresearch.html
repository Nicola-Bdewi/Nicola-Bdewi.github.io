Survey Questions (given to university students)

  1. Age of the elderly.
  2. Gender of the elderly.
  3. Relationship to the university student (parent, grandparent,...).
  4. General health status (good, fair, poor).
  5. Familiarity with using apps and technology (Likert scale from 'Very Unfamiliar' to 'Very Familiar').
  6. How frequently do you visit healthcare providers? (Multiple choice: Monthly, Quarterly, Semi-annually, Annually, Rarely).
  7. Importance of having medication reminders in an app (Likert scale).
  8. How would you rate your interest in mental health resources? (Likert scale).
  9. Types of mental health resources interested in (Multiple choice: Meditation guides, Therapist directories, Stress management tools, etc.).
  10. Ease of scheduling doctor appointments (Likert scale).
  11. Preferences for virtual vs. in-person appointments (Binary: Yes/No, with a follow-up question if yes).
  12. Level of interest in activities such as exercise, social events, learning new skills (Likert scale for each activity type).
  13. Preferences for receiving activity suggestions (Multiple choice: Daily, Weekly, Monthly, Not interested).
  14. What extra features would you want in a health and wellness app?
  15. Do you have any more comments or suggestions for enhancing healthcare access or management through technology?
